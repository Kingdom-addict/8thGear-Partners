8thGear Growth as a Service

Small and Growing businesses in Nigeria are challenged with limited access to Business information, Professional advice, Basic infrastructure, funding, and a safe space to experiment with ideas. At 8thGear we offer growth as a service. It provides a platform of support so that business owners can be connected to various resources to support their business growth. Our team is experienced in Growth engineering, Enterprise support, and business advisory. We have and still support a wide range of businesses.

8thGear Academy

We provide training for both existing and new employees for optimal performance. Right now, Young people have trouble with getting and creating jobs. We fix that by upskilling them with soft and technical/digital skills so that they are able to secure or create Jobs. Our team is made up of HR, Training, Project, and Enterprise development professionals.

8thGear Co-working Space

The 8thGear Co-working space was established for growing businesses that would rather focus on their core value and avoid the distraction of managing leased real estate.
