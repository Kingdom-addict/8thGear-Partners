WHO WE ARE?

We are youth/female-led & 100% Nigerian owned/managed. Our obsession is Job placements, capability/Skills development and enterprise development for youths in Nigeria and their growing businesses.

Beyond our profit motive is a social mission built into our business model. Giving young people 'a chance at meaningfully contributing to their community with dignity' - is a significant part of what we do.
